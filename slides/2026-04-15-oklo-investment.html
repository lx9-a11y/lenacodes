Company Overview

Compact fast-fission nuclear reactors for data centers & remote grids

Oklo designs, manufactures, and operates the Aurora powerhouse — a sub-500MW small modular reactor (SMR) that sits on a small footprint, requires minimal transmission infrastructure, and sells power directly to customers under long-term agreements (15–20 year PPAs).

The Bull Case

  • AI data centers need massive, reliable baseload power — nuclear is the only 24/7 zero-carbon option
  • Liberty Energy partnership for hybrid gas-to-nuclear transition
  • DOE loan application in progress for Aurora manufacturing
  • Regulatory engagement with NRC — pre-application meetings active
  • Power purchase agreements target $0.05–0.10/kWh range, competitive with gas

Risk Factors

  • No commercial reactors operational yet — pre-revenue
  • NRC design certification process is long and uncertain
  • Stock highly volatile — down ~33% from 52-week high
  • High-risk, high-reward: suitable only for aggressive growth allocation
  • Dependence on a small number of prospective customers
